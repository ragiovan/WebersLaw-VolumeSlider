How to Use:

Press button on the volume slider to be either linear or logarithmic. Increase volume and see how you perceieve the sound.

<img width="520" height="450" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4b31d023-7bac-4860-9cdb-28aba726df55" />

Note, a logarithmic volume slider refers to the scale, not the operation performed on the linear. An exponential operation is performed on the linear to cancel out
the "logarithmic perception" of our ears. Together, they form a more smooth linear scale to our ears. ie, e<sup>lnx</sup> = x.
